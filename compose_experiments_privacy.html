Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Overview

Compose Experiments ("the App") is a collection of experimental user interface demonstrations built with Jetpack Compose. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information when you use our App.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track user behavior
  • Access device identifiers
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require user accounts or registration

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions solely for demonstrating specific UI features:

  • Internet Access: Only if required for specific experiments (e.g., loading sample images)
  • Storage Access: Only if required for specific experiments (e.g., saving generated content)

All permissions are used exclusively within the App for demonstration purposes and no data is transmitted externally.

Data Storage

Any data created within the App (such as drawings, animations, or interactions) is:

  • Stored temporarily in device memory during app usage
  • Automatically cleared when the App is closed
  • Never transmitted outside your device

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any advertising networks. We use Google Play Store services and Google SDKs or any Android related services where needed for app functionality.

Children's Privacy and Safety

Age-Appropriate Content: The App is suitable for all ages. All content consists exclusively of technical UI demonstrations and contains no inappropriate material for children.

No Data Collection: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13.

No Advertising: The App contains no advertisements or marketing content of any kind.

Legal Compliance: The App is designed to comply with all applicable children's privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union
  • Age-appropriate design codes and similar regulations worldwide

Parental Assurance: Parents can be confident that this App is safe for children as it:

  • Functions entirely offline (except for optional sample content loading)
  • Contains no social features or user interaction
  • Has no in-app purchases or premium features
  • Includes only educational technical content

Open Source

This App is an open-source project. The source code is publicly available and can be reviewed to verify our privacy practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

  • GitHub Issues on our repository: https://github.com/MaTriXy/Compose-experiments/issues

Consent

By using the App, you agree to this privacy policy.
